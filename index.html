About me

I always loved playing games (but not loosing). Making games has been an interst since the golden era of Commodore 64.

I like all kind of games, but retro 2d games is what I have most experience in making, and they also relate the most to me.

My goal is to develop fun casual games which will teach the player about science, history or some other interesting topic.

Games

Crabs'n'Sticks A maze game set on the beach. Play as an octopus catching crabs for dinner.
Samurai Palace Kill the guards and the samurai. Based on a true story from feudal Japan.
Panic in the Pantanal Fire is raging in the Pantanal. You are the only one who can save the animals.
